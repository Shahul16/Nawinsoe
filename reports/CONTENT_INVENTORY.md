## All Countries/Destinations

### United Kingdom 🇬🇧
- Tuition: £15,000 - £40,000 per year
- Duration: 1-2 years Masters, 3 years Bachelors
- Visa: Student visa (Tier 4) with post-study work rights
- Benefits: Globally recognized degrees, 1-2 year Masters programs, Work visa opportunities, Multicultural community, Oxford/Cambridge/Russell Group

### Canada 🇨🇦
- Tuition: CAD 15,000 - 30,000 per year
- Duration: 1-2 years Masters, 4 years Bachelors
- Visa: Study permit with PGWP pathway to PR
- Benefits: Affordable compared to UK/US, Post-graduation work permits, Safety, Pathway to permanent residency

### Australia 🇦🇺
- Tuition: AUD 20,000 - 45,000 per year
- Duration: 1.5-2 years Masters, 3 years Bachelors
- Visa: Student visa with work rights and pathways to PR
- Benefits: High-ranked universities, Great weather, Work rights, Skill migration opportunities

### Ireland 🇮🇪
- Tuition: €8,000 - 25,000 per year
- Duration: 1-2 years Masters, 3-4 years Bachelors
- Visa: Student visa with work rights
- Benefits: EU location, Strong tech industry, Work rights, Pathway to EU residence

### Europe (Broader) 🇪🇺
- Tuition: €5,000 - 30,000 per year
- Duration: 1-2 years Masters, 3-4 years Bachelors
- Visa: Country-specific student visas
- Benefits: Diverse education systems, Multiple countries, Erasmus+ opportunities
